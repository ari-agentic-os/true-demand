# 08 · Behavioral experiments (the truest bill is a real action, not an interview)

Even a perfect Mom Test interview is still optimized **"say."** The strongest viability evidence is a real
commitment — money first, then time/reputation. So every kickoff pairs the interview guide with **ONE
behavioral experiment** matched to the riskiest assumption, run in parallel with the interviews.

## The evidence ladder (weak → strong)

1. Opinion ("nice idea") — worthless
2. Stated intent ("I'd pay") — near-worthless
3. Past behavior (Mom Test: what they *already* paid for) — good
4. Small commitment now (gives email/time, joins a waitlist, books the call) — better
5. Money commitment (pre-order, deposit, card on file) — strong
6. Recurring real charge (they keep paying) — proof

Push every claim one rung down toward "did."

## Pick ONE experiment by stage (match it to the riskiest assumption)

- **Pre-MVP / concept risk:** landing-page smoke test (real CTA → measure click→signup), fake-door, waitlist
  with a small deposit, concierge (deliver the value by hand for 1–2 users).
- **Have MVP, can't-charge / low-activation risk (the core case):**
  - **Pre-sell / paid pilot** — "上线发你，先付/押一点" / "pay now, I'll onboard you" (strongest).
  - **Real payment link / Stripe** — put the price up; measure *checkout-start → paid*, not page views.
  - **Fake-door on the paid feature** — a locked "一键同步 / Pro" button; count who hits it before it exists.
  - **Founder-led concierge upsell** — offer the paid outcome by hand to 5 engaged users; does anyone pay?

## Design rules (so it's evidence, not theater)

- **One falsifiable metric + a pass/fail threshold set BEFORE you run it** (e.g. "≥3 of 15 engaged users start checkout").
- **Real friction** — ask for the card / the deposit. Frictionless "interest" is just traffic.
- Small but real n; never mistake traffic / upvotes for demand.
- **Pair with interviews:** the experiment gives the *did*, the interview explains the *why*.

## Output (write into the action plan)

ONE experiment: the assumption it tests · the exact setup · the single metric · the pass/fail threshold.
Goes in `行动计划_招募与实验.md` / `plan_recruit_experiment.md`, run alongside the interviews. Log any money
commitment as the highest-confidence viability evidence for `/diagnose`.
